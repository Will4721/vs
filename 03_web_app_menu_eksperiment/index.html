Lore

"Kratos is a powerful warrior with super human strength from the land of Sparta in Greece during a time of gods and conflict. Kratos becomes a powerful warlord who also has a family, a wife and a daughter, whom he loves very much. Unfortunetly in a great battle, he is forced to sell his soul to the God of War, Ares, to win. Ares then enslaves him to do his bidding, leading him to ultimately kill his family by accident. In vengeance Kratos opens Pandora's Box which was guarded by the Titan Cronos, the enslaved father of Zeus, to obtain power to kill Ares, becoming the new God of War. His anger has not subsided though, and he rampages through Greece with his armies. Zeus, lord of the Pantheon and leader of the Greek Gods as well as revealed to be the father of Kratos, kills Kratos to end his menace, to which Kratos swears revenge. Kratos escapes hell and travels back in time by defeating and taking advantage of the Sisters of Fate who had pre-determined Kratos' destiny to die at the hands of Zeus in order to undo the events of his death. Kratos then battles his father and defeats him. Zeus escapes to the Pantheon however and Kratos awakens the Titans of old (celestials who gave birth to the gods and were later userped by said gods, namely Zeus) with the help of the mother of all Titans, Gaia, to try and destory the Pantheon. "
